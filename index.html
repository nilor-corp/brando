Brando: AI-Powered Visuals for Unforgettable Spaces.

BRANDO is nilor studio's tool for making stunning, on-brand content for brand activation in any venue, instantly. Turn your unique screen layouts into captivating, branded experiences.

The Challenge: Blank Canvases

Modern venues, especially immersive ones, have vast and complex screen real estate. Creating custom branded visuals for these unique layouts is traditionally slow, complex, and expensive for both the venue and the client.

  • ✓ Unique screen dimensions & layouts
  • ✓ High cost of bespoke content creation
  • ✓ Long turnaround times for visuals

The Solution: Brando

We take your brand assets—logos, colors, and themes—and use Brando to generate dynamic, multi-screen content that perfectly fits your space. The result is a seamless, branded video with true spatial continuity.

  • ✓ Automated content generation
  • ✓ Fast, cost-effective, and scalable
  • ✓ Unlimited variety of moods and styles

How It Works

1.

Input Assets

We take your screen layouts, resolutions, logos, and thematic imagery as input.

2.

Generate Video

Brando generates a base video that works across all canvases simultaneously, ensuring continuity.

3.

Upscale & Detail

We perform multiple rounds of upscaling to treat each screen at high resolution and add incredible detail.
